### LEC 13

5.  Product rule

$$$
\frac{d}{dx} \left[ f(x)\cdot g(x) \right] =\frac{d}{dx} [f(x)]\cdot g(x)+ f(x)\cdot \frac{d}{dx} [g(x)]
$$$

#### Example 1
Find $$F'(x)$$ if $$F(x)=(6x^3)(7x^4)$$.

6. Quotient rule

$$$
\frac{d}{dx} \left[ \frac{f(x)}{g(x)} \right] =\frac{\frac{d}{dx} [f(x)]\cdot g(x)- f(x)\cdot \frac{d}{dx} [g(x)]}{[g(x)]^2}
$$$
