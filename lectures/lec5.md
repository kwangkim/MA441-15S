### Direct substitution Property

If \\(f\\) is a polynomial or a rational function or radical function and a is **in the domain** of \\(f\\),

\\[ \lim_ {x\to a} f(x)=f(a) \\]

### Example 3.

### Fact

If \\( f(x)=g(x) \\) when \\( x\neq a\\), then

\\[ \lim _ {x \to a} f(x)
=\lim_ {x\to a} f(x) \\]
if the limits exist.
