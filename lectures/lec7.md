## Lec 7 1.8 continuity.

A function $f$ is continuous at a number $a$ if
$$
\lim_{x\to a}f(x)=f(a)
$$
Otherwise, we say $f$ is discontinuous at $a$
#### Remark.

1. $f(a)$ is defined.
2. $\lim_{x\to a}f(x)$ exists
3. $\lim_{x\to a}f(x)=f(a)$
